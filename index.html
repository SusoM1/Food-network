About Me

Successful restaurants are, first and foremost, about the food. Your restaurant should have a core group of recipes that please your customers and can be prepared by the kitchen staff. You may also choose to offer changing specials that provide variety for both your staff and your customers. Ingredients should be affordable and purchased in quantities you can move quickly enough to maintain freshness, but not so quickly that you run out.
